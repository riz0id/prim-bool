* Removes the `Int16#` comparisons `ltInt16#`, `leInt16#`, `eqInt16#`, `neInt16#`, `gtInt16#`, and `geInt16#`.
* Removes the `Int32#` comparisons `ltInt32#`, `leInt32#`, `eqInt32#`, `neInt32#`, `gtInt32#`, and `geInt32#`.
